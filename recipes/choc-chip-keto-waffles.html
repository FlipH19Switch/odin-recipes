Chocolate Chip Keto Waffles

Description

This recipe takes 15 minutes to make and will provide up to four waffles if you have a small waffle-maker. You will need four total ingredients. It is unclear whether cooked waffles can be safely stored in the refrigerator.

Ingredients

  • 64g micellar casein (~2 scoops)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp semi-sweet chocolate baking chips (e.g. Lily's)

Steps

  1. Melt the butter in the microwave.
  2. Whisk the eggs in with the butter.
  3. Mix in the protein powder.
  4. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  5. Add the mixture to your waffle-maker of choice. Remove waffles when they begin browning on the surface.
