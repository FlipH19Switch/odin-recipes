Keto Ice Cream

This recipe takes only five minutes to make an produces six servings of ice cream. It requires seven ingredients that are common to any keto household. You can store the leftovers in an airtight container in your freezer for up to six months.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup full-fat coconut milk
  • 3/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup almond butter
  • 3.5 ounces cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup erythritol
  • 1 tsp almond extract
  • 1/2 cup toasted almond slices

Steps

  1. Add the heavy cream to large mixing bowl and whisk until you see peaks.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk the cream cheese and erythritol together.
  3. Add this cream cheese and erythritol mixture to the heavy cream and mix.
  4. Mix in the almond butter, coconut milk, and almond extract.
  5. Sprinkle the toasted almonds on top of the mixture.
  6. Freeze the mixture overnight before serving.
